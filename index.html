Wisaadhome Blog

Wisaadhome is a digital platform designed to make it easy for users to buy, sell, or rent properties. Whether you are a property owner looking to sell or rent a house, apartment, or land, or a property seeker searching for a home or investment, Wisaadhome provides everything you need.

On Wisaadhome, users can:

  • Offer Properties: Post your property listings with complete information and high-quality photos to attract potential buyers or renters.
  • Find Properties: Browse available property listings with various filters such as location, price, and property type to find one that fits your needs.
  • Direct Contact: Messaging and calling features that make it easy to communicate between property owners and potential buyers or renters.
  • Tips and Articles: Get the latest information on property market trends, tips for sellers and buyers, and guides on the buying, selling, and renting process.

Wisaadhome aims to be a trusted source and an easy solution for all your property needs, with a user-friendly interface and a transparent process. Join our community and easily and quickly find or offer your dream property.

Baiklist

Baiklist is a to-do list application that helps users manage their daily tasks with features like filtering by date and task status.

Key features of Baiklist include:

  • User-Friendly Interface: A clean and easy-to-use design ensures an enjoyable user experience.
  • Task Grouping: Users can group tasks by categories, frequency, status, calendar, or deadlines.
  • Reminders and Notifications: The reminder feature helps users stay on track by sending notifications for important tasks.
  • Calendar Integration: Tasks can be integrated with a calendar to provide an overview of the schedule.
  • Dark/Light Mode: Options for dark or light mode for visual comfort based on user preferences.

Baiklist helps users stay organized and productive with a simple and easy-to-use interface.
